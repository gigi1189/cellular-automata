Cellular Automata Sim i
TThis is a simple tool for exploring cellular automata. The idea is that each cell lives on a grid, and whether it’s on or off (alive or dead, 1 or 0) depends on its previous state and the states of its neighboring cells.

This simulation lets you switch between four different rule sets (including one I made), visualize cell age, and track density. You can also interact with it directly by drawing on the grid yourself. Try it out!

Each living cell has a “lifetime counter.” When a cell is born, it starts at 0. Every surviving step increases its age. Warm colors represent new life and will turn cooler as they get older. Leaving this box unchecked will simply show "alive" or "dead" cells, with no in-between.
